Discover Los Angeles Redesign

Short tagline

Project Overview

DiscoverLosAngeles.com is the official guide to Los Angeles, and aims to grow LA’s visitor economy by providing users with information on LA’s events, attractions, and activities. This project re-imagined the website’s existing user flow for searching and finding an event to attend, while also giving the site and its navigation a facelift.

Role: UX/UI Designer
Tools: Pen & Paper, Whiteboard, LucidChart, Sketch, InVision (use icons maybe?)
Timeframe: 3 weeks
