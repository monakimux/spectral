Personal Finance App

Strategized and designed a personal financial app

Project Overview

After creating various financial apps targeting different audiences, an all-in-one financial management app was proposed as the next product.

Role: UX & UI Designer
Platform: iOS
Tools: Pen & Paper, Whiteboard, Sketch

Challenges
  • Most consumers don't understand the impact their credit score has on their financial life
  • Consumers are not sure if they should trust personal finance apps because they are not sure if the benefits of the app justifies the risk of entering their personal information
Solutions
  • Make credit scores easier to understand
  • Be transparent about how information will be used

Research

Competitive Audit

To get a better understanding of what was already on the market, I conducted a competitive audit of various financial apps. I took note of the following:

  • Available platforms
  • Number of users
  • Rankings
  • Ratings

I also went through each app screen by screen to see how each handled things like sign up flow, navigation, home screen, and information architecture.

Additional Research

Since we were already contracted to use a particular credit reporting agency's service, I spent quite a bit of time to fully understand their terminology and requirements. I read through their blogs to understand how they explained credit scores and their products to their audience. I also studied their documentation on their service to fully understand what inputs, outputs, and errors it provided. This helped me to look for possible ways to simplify the information for our users.
